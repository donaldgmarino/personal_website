Simcoe APA #4 :: 12/10/2011



Ingredients for 5 gallons
  • 1lb. British Carastan Malt Barley
  • 1lb. 30L Crystal Malt Barley
  • 9lb. Cooper's Light Malt Extract
  • 3.0 oz. Simcoe Whole Flower Hops
  • 1.5 oz. Cascase Whole Flower Hops
  • 1 vial White Labs live yeast culture - California V WLP051

Procedure
Heat 2.5 gallons water slowly to about 180° F with the grain steeping in a grain bag. When mixture reaches 180° or so, remove grain bag, allowing wort to drain out of grain bag. Cast aside grain bag and bring mixture slowly to a boil. When wort begins to boil, remove from heat and add the liquid malt extract. When extract has dissolved, add the Simcoe hopss and return to heat. Boil for one hour at rolling boil. When boil time is 3 min shy of 1 hour, add the whole Cascade hops and stir for the remaining 3 minutes. Remove from heat and strain as much hop pellet and hops as is pracitcal. Run wort chiller for ~20 minutes. Pitch the yeast when wort reaches room temperature and affix the airlock.
