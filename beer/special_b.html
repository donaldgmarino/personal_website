American Special B Ale :: February 4th, 2011


O.G. 1.039
F.G. 1.010
ABV 3.2%

Ingredients for 5 gallons
  • 14 oz. American Pale Malt (crushed)
  • 4 oz. 50-65 Crystal Malt (crushed)
  • 8 oz. Special B Malt (crushed)
  • ~4 lb. Shop Bulk Light Malt Extract Syrup
  • 2.0 lb. Breiss Dry Light Malt Extract
  • ~1 oz. Malto-Dextrin
  • 1 oz. Cascade Hop Pellets
  • 1 oz. Citra Hop Pellets
  • 1 oz. Simcoe Hop Pellets
  • 1 vial White Labs live yeast culture - California Ale WLP001

Procedure
Heat 2.5 gallons water slowly to about 180° F with the grain steeping in a grain bag. When mixture reaches 180° or so, remove grain bag, allowing wort to drain out of grain bag. Cast aside grain bag and bring mixture slowly to a boil. When wort begins to boil, remove from heat and add the liquid malt extract and the dry malt extract. When extract has dissolved, add the Citra hop pellets and return to heat. Boil gently for 30 minutes and add the Simcoe hop pellets. Boil for 27 more minutes (57 total) and add the Cascade pellets. Only boil Cascade hops for final 2-3 minutes of boil. Remove from heat and run wort chiller for ~20 minutes. Pitch the yeast when wort reaches room temperature and affix the airlock.

Tasting Notes
This beer turned out fantastic, one of the best yet! Had a party where we drank this all in an evening. Beer was very well received, would definitely say this was good stuff!
